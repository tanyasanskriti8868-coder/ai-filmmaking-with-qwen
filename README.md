# 🎬 The Last File

> *A sci-fi thriller created entirely using AI-powered filmmaking tools.*
## 📖 Overview

**The Last File** is a short AI-generated sci-fi thriller that follows three investigators—**AIRA**, **DEV**, and **MAYA**—as they uncover a mysterious file scheduled for deletion. What begins as a routine investigation quickly turns into a race against time involving erased memories, hidden archives, impossible recordings, and a truth that was never meant to be discovered.

As the story unfolds, the trio realizes that they were not trapped inside the facility. Instead, they had willingly erased their own memories to protect a dangerous secret. Now they must recover the truth and reveal it to the world before it disappears forever.

---

## 🎥 Project Summary

A mysterious file. A forgotten truth. A countdown that changes everything.

When AIRA, DEV, and MAYA discover a secret file moments away from deletion, they are pulled into a chilling investigation filled with hidden archives, impossible recordings, and memories they don't remember losing. As reality begins to unravel, they uncover a shocking truth: they once exposed a dangerous secret and erased their own memories to protect it. Now, with the truth on the verge of disappearing forever, they must finish what they started.

## 🛠️ Production Workflow

### 1. Story Development

* Created original screenplay
* Divided story into 12 scenes
* Planned dialogue and suspense elements

### 2. Character Design

* Generated character references
* Maintained visual consistency across scenes
* Created unique appearances for each character

### 3. Scene Generation

* Generated scenes individually
* Used detailed prompts for every scene
* Refined outputs through multiple iterations

### 4. Prompt Engineering

Focused on:

* Character consistency
* Voice generation
* Camera movements
* Environment continuity
* Emotional expressions

### 5. Video Assembly

* Exported individual clips
* Arranged scenes chronologically
* Added transitions and final edits
* Rendered final movie

## ⚡ Challenges Faced

### Character Consistency

Maintaining the same appearance across multiple AI-generated scenes.

### Voice Generation

Ensuring characters spoke clearly and followed the intended dialogue.

### Lip Synchronization

Aligning speech with facial movements.

### Environment Continuity

Keeping rooms and locations visually consistent.

### Camera Control

Creating smooth cinematic movements while maintaining scene coherence.

## 💡 Key Learnings

* AI filmmaking is more than generating videos.
* Prompt engineering plays a critical role in output quality.
* Character consistency requires detailed planning.
* Scene-by-scene generation provides better control.
* Iteration and refinement are essential parts of the creative process.

---

## 🎬 Behind the Project

This project was created as an experiment to explore how far free AI tools can be pushed in filmmaking. Rather than generating random clips, the goal was to create a coherent narrative with recurring characters, consistent environments, emotional storytelling, and a satisfying conclusion.

The project demonstrates how AI can assist creators in transforming ideas into visual stories without traditional filmmaking resources.
